Frequently Asked Questions

Please visit the Brooklyn Campus Library on schedule. Upon arriving at the building, have your Pratt ID card ready. You will call the desk via the number listed on the door using your own cellphone (preferred) OR you may buzz in via the pad at the door. After verification of your appointment time, you will be granted entry to the lobby and a staff member will instruct you how to locate and pick up your reserved equipment.

Directions to Brooklyn Campus

Once approved, the pickup appointment can take as little as a few minutes. Please show up to the library at the beginning of your appointment reservation. Be sure to wear a mask, stand six feet apart, and observe Pratt's Campus Access Checklist.

Equipment returns must be scheduled in advance and delivered in person. Please schedule a time to return your equipment before or on the due date. Return Equipment

You will receive an email confirming your appointment time. This email will contain a link to cancel your appointment if needed. From there, you can schedule another pickup time from the link in your initial email notice, email subject: "Circulation Notice from Pratt Libraries." If you want to cancel a hold, please reach out to us at equipment.library@pratt.edu or call (718) 636-3626.

If you need equipment over the weekend, please make sure to place your pickup appointment no later than 11:00AM on Friday.

If you think there might be a problem with your request, please check the following:

  • Make sure that emails from the libraries did not go to the spam folder (to be sure you always receive these, add "equipment.library@pratt.edu" and "alerts@mail.libcal.com" to your email contact list)
  • If you have recently made your request for equipment or for a pickup appointment, give it a bit of time. Each step takes a little while.
  • If it has been more than two business days and you have not received any updates, please reach out to us at equipment.library@pratt.edu and let us know and we will assist you.
