Once approved, the pickup appointment can take as little as a few minutes. Please show up to the library at the beginning of your appointment reservation. Be sure to wear a mask, stand six feet apart, and observe Pratt's Campus Access Checklist.

Books & DVDs
You can return your books to the book drop in front of the library in Brooklyn, or in front of the 4th floor library at PMC. If you are in the city but not able to come onto campus, you can return your books to the public safety officer at the 200 Willoughby entrance to the Brooklyn campus, or at the entrance desk at PMC. Or you can mail them to us at the following address. The "media mail" rate at the US Post Office, will probably be your least expensive option!

Pratt Institute Libraries
200 Willoughby Avenue
Brooklyn, NY 11205

If you have any questions or if your situation isn't covered by these options please feel free to contact us using the library chat service, or email libref@pratt.edu.

Equipment

Equipment returns must be scheduled in advance and delivered in person. Please schedule a time to return your equipment before or on the due date. Return Equipment

You will receive an email confirming your appointment time. This email will contain a link to cancel your appointment if needed. From there, you can schedule another pickup time from the link in your initial email notice, email subject: "Circulation Notice from Pratt Libraries." If you want to cancel a hold on an item, please reach out to us at libref@pratt.edu.

You can pickup as many items as you have requested and are confirmed available for pickup. Feel free to request several items on our catalog and wait to receive email confirmations that they are all available. Then click the link in the email notification and select one pickup time.
